About Mathematical App

This app is all about mathematics, so this app basically do it gives tutorial lessons to the students who struggles with mathematics. Especially to those 'academically' challenged learners ..who finds the math subject difficult. (As we all know that that most of south African students especially in our townships).

How it Works

Teacher's or educator's who knows and understand maths can post videos of themselves teaching maths.They'll get paid by the number of views and ratings meaning they'll be paid through each subscriber. It will be charged per download and payed per click.
